EC Grill Chicken Ati-Atihan

OUR DELICIOUS STORY

The EC Grill Chicken At-Atihan derived its name from the Ati-Atihan Festival, as well as its festive slogan: Sasayaw ka sa sarap! (You'll dance because it's so delicious!).

The Ati-Atihan Festival is held on the third Sunday of January in Kalibo, Aklan, Philippines on the island of Panay. The name Ati-Atihan means “to be like Atis” or “make believe Atis,” the local name for the Aetas, who are indigenous settlers in Panay and other parts of the Philippines.

ABOUT CHICKEN ATI-ATIHAN

Our mission is to serve authentic Filipino dishes within the Laoag City area. EC Grill Chicken Ati-Atihan is a place to celebrate special moments by offering the best food, service, and a delightful yet festive experience.
